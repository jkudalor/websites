Springland International School's 3rd Graduation

By Wisdom Dickson -Parent Springland Interantional School's 3rd Annual graduation on July 28, 2018, at the premises of the school near the NDC Bus Stop, Klagon. The theme for this year's graduation ceremony was "Education, The priceless Heritage for a child".

It was an amzing graduation ceremony as children entertained their parents and guests with several captivating poetry and Bible recitals, drama and modelling.

Our grauduants were clad in resplendent uniform robes as they brimmed with excitement and confidence. During the occassion, they were given words of advice from the Guest Speaker, Mrs. Angela Carmen- Appiah as they graduated from Kindergarten to primary school.

The 3rd Springland International School graduation was indeed a success, a great achievement, a befitting improvenent of two previous graduations.

Nevertheless, there is still room for improvement as we preapre towards our next graduation, the next step towards orur goal of excellence.
